Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about prompt engineering courses
What is prompt engineering and why is it important?
Prompt engineering is the skill of effectively communicating with AI systems like ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini to get accurate, useful outputs. As AI becomes central to business operations, employees who can effectively prompt AI are 40% more productive. It's becoming as essential as email skills were in the 1990s.
Which prompt engineering course is best for beginners?
For beginners, Iternal AI Academy offers the most comprehensive starting point with 810+ courses covering every skill level. The Vanderbilt/Coursera course is also excellent for those wanting university credentials. Both include hands-on practice, which is essential for learning.
How much do prompt engineering courses cost?
Prices range from free (PMI course, Learn Prompting intro) to $399 (Blockchain Council certification). Subscription platforms like Coursera cost $49-59/month. Iternal AI Academy offers the best value at $199 one-time for 810+ courses with lifetime access.
Are prompt engineering certificates worth it?
Yes, for career advancement. Verified certificates demonstrate AI competency to employers. However, hands-on skills matter more than paper credentials. Choose courses that offer both interactive practice AND certificates, like Iternal AI Academy or the Vanderbilt course.
Can I learn prompt engineering for free?
Yes, several free options exist: PMI's "Talking to AI" course (free with PDUs), Learn Prompting's intro content, and DataCamp's basic tier. However, free courses are limited in scope. For comprehensive training, paid options provide significantly more value.
What's the difference between subscription and perpetual license courses?
Subscription courses (Coursera, Learn Prompting) charge monthly ($21-59/month), meaning costs accumulate over time. Perpetual licenses (Iternal AI Academy $199, Udemy courses) are one-time payments with lifetime access. For long-term learning, perpetual licenses save 70%+ over 2 years.
Which course is best for enterprise teams?
Iternal AI Academy is purpose-built for enterprise with volume discounts (30 seats for $2,500), progress tracking, and verified certificates. Coursera offers business plans but at higher per-seat costs. Most other courses lack enterprise licensing options.
Do prompt engineering skills work across different AI tools?
Yes, core prompting principles transfer across ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other AI tools. Iternal AI Academy specifically teaches AI-agnostic techniques that work everywhere, while some courses focus on specific tools like Google Prompting Essentials.
